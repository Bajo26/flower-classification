Welcome to the Flower Plant Health Classification Tutorial

Follow these steps to learn how to use the application:

Capture Image

Click the "Capture Image" button to capture a frame from the camera feed.

Switch Camera

Use the "Switch Camera" button to switch between available cameras.

Uploading from Local File

Click the "Go To Upload Local Image" button to choose an image from your local files and upload it.

Classifying the Uploaded Image

After uploading the image, the application will automatically classify the plant health.
